Nicole has spent a majority of her career teaching Fine Arts at one of the largest school districts in the State of Maine. After relocating to San Francisco, she began pursuing a career in Design. Her love for startups lead her to become the Founder and UX/UI designer for the San Francisco based startup, Ovooko. There she designed the company's identity and brand, the website's design, user interface and user experience, as well as the site's marketing publications and email correspondence. When Nicole is not out chasing the snow, she continues to collaborate with area businesses, providing them with inspiration and focus for their brand.
